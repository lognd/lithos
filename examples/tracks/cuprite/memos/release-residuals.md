doc(memos/release-residuals.md)` deviations across this directory's
single-file track designs. Every waived obligation is a recorded
machinery wall, not a design failure; the per-claim basis line names
the wall verbatim. The dominant classes:

  (fea_static_stress, fea_modal, ...) are honestly unmatched in the
  release environment (WO-27 uninstalled posture).

  (comparator/temporal/optimize walls; translate() defers).

- Interface/import conformance edges with no scalar window (D195.3),
