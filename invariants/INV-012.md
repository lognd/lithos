Checked mirror of INV-12 (Waiver honesty) in
`docs/spec/regolith/13-invariants.md`; that file is the normative
source.

Mechanism: `classify` matches a declared waiver against the obligation
set and records the exact match set; a waiver matching nothing is
`Stale` (an error), never a silent absorption.

Argument summary: every waiver consequence lands on an auditable
surface (the recorded-match-set diff), so growth of a match set or a
newly-uncovered failure cannot hide behind an existing waiver.
