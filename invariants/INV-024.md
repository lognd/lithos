Checked mirror of INV-24 (Release-gate totality) in
`docs/spec/regolith/13-invariants.md`; that file is the normative
source.

Mechanism: `release_gate` enumerates the closed set of failure-
acceptance mechanisms (`todo!`, `assume!`, `waive`/deviations, per-item
CLI acknowledgments) against the evidence set, and consumes the
payload's `WaiveLedger` for accepted deviations.

Argument summary: INV-2 guarantees no mechanism hides a failure by
re-labeling; this invariant guarantees the *gate* sees every remaining
failure -- together, a green release means proven or explicitly
accepted, with nothing in between.
