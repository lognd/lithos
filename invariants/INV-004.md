`docs/spec/regolith/13-invariants.md`; that file is the normative
source.

Mechanism: the artifact group is the intersection of per-construct
*declared* contributions (conservative by construction, computed by
`SymmetryGroup::intersect`); a symmetry-breaking construct collapses
the orbit to `Trivial` rather than asserting a group that no longer
holds.

Argument summary: intersection of sound under-approximations is a
sound under-approximation; the givens-invariance check (in
`regolith-lower/src/ownership.rs`) closes the asymmetric-load hole.
