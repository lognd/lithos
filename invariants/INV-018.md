Checked mirror of INV-18 (Reference determinism) in
`docs/spec/regolith/13-invariants.md`; that file is the normative
source.

Mechanism: `resolve_decl` classifies every `refer` per cardinality
typing (`Entity`/`Set[;n]`/`Set` with `.all`/`.only`/`.any`); an
over-match or under-match is E0301, never a tie-break heuristic.

Argument summary: the resolver either produces a unique answer
consistent with the declared cardinality or fails constructively --
there is no tie-break path.

Anchor (primary enforcing site): `crates/regolith-lower/src/query.rs :: resolve_decl`
