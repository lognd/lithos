Checked mirror of INV-9 (Corner conservatism) in
`docs/spec/regolith/13-invariants.md`; that file is the normative
source.

Mechanism: `Model.discharge` computes the claim's quantity at its
worst corner via the model's own corner map; interval arithmetic in
budgets/ledgers is outward-rounding.

Argument summary (honesty note carried over from the spec): this
reduces to a *per-model obligation*, not a global proof -- a model
with a wrong corner map is unsound. This is deliberately the weakest
link in the chain.
